Benefits of Chamomile Tea: Soothes cold symptoms Helps with Digestion Good for sleep

Chamomile tea

Chamomile tea is a herbal tea that is made from the dried flowers of the chamomile plant. It is popular for its calming and soothing properties and many take it before they sleep. This tea has several benefits including helping colds, helping ease digestion, and its most known benefit helping with sleep. It has a mild floral taste and is often paired with lemon and honey. Despite being a tea, it is caffeine-free and is a great choice if you want a relaxing beverage.
